Restrict a polynomial to a given line.

Given two points $b,c \in \mathbb{F}^{\log n}$ create a line $l$ such that $l(0) = b$ and $l(1) = c$ and restrict an MLE $\tilde{W}$ to this line outputting a univariate polynomial.
